Strawberry Mango Chia Popsicles

Makes: 10 servings - Serving size: 1 popsicle

Note:

This is a treat and is not counted as part of your meal. Enjoy treats in moderation.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1-1/2 cups strawberries
  • 1-1/2 cups mango pieces
  • 1-1/4 cups pure coconut water
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 4 tsp chia seeds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Puree strawberries with half of the coconut water in a blender or food processor. Add 1 tsp honey and 2 tsp chia seeds. Set aside.
  2. Puree mango in a blender or food processor with remaining coconut water, honey, and chia seeds.
  3. Alternatively pour each fruit mixture (for a marbled look) into popsicle molds. Freeze until set.
