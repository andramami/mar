Cinnamon Baked Pears

Makes: 4 servings - Serving size: 1/2 pear

Each Serving Contains: 0 Proteins, 1 Carbohydrate, 0 Vegetables, 0 Fat

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 pears, any variety
  • 4 tsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Slice pears in half, lengthwise, and remove seeds. Arrange them face up on a baking tray.
  3. Drizzle each slice with 1 tsp maple syrup, brushing over the face of the pear and leaving some inside the center.
  4. Divide walnuts evenly into pear centers. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  5. Bake 20-30 minutes, until pears are tender and cooked through. Serve warm.
