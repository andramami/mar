Blueberry Coconut Breakfast Bowl

Makes: 1 serving

Each Serving Contains: 2 Proteins, 1 Carbohydrate, 0 Vegetables, 1 Fat

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup ice
  • 1/4 cup frozen blueberries
  • 3 Tbsp unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 3 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 oz sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Blend almond milk, ice & blueberries in a blender until smooth.
  2. Add coconut and blend until well incorporated.
  3. Pour into a serving bowl and top with chia seeds, strawberries & sliced almonds. Serve immediately.
